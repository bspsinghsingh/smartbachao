पेट्रोल बचाने के smart तरीके

Smart ways to reduce fuel costs

1
Short distances के लिए cycle
Cycle for short distances
5-7 km के अंदर की distances के लिए cycle use करें। एक बार invest करो, सालों तक fuel बचाओ।
Use a cycle for distances within 5-7 km. One-time investment, years of fuel savings.
2
Carpooling करें
Carpool to work
Same direction में जाने वाले colleagues के साथ carpool करें। Fuel और parking दोनों बचते हैं।
Carpool with colleagues going the same direction. Saves both fuel and parking costs.
3
CNG conversion (अगर suitable हो)
CNG conversion (if suitable)
CNG पेट्रोल से काफी सस्ती होती है। अगर आपके route पर CNG stations हैं तो conversion consider करें।
CNG is significantly cheaper than petrol. If CNG stations are on your route, consider conversion.
4
EV consider करें
Consider EV
अगर daily range 50-80 km है और home charging possible है, तो EV long-term में बहुत बचत करता है।
If your daily range is 50-80 km and home charging is possible, an EV saves significantly in the long run.

Petrol बचाने के 10 Smart तरीके

10 Smart Ways to Save Petrol

1
Tyre pressure सही रखें
Keep Tyre Pressure Correct
Under-inflated tyres 5-10% ज्यादा fuel खाते हैं। हर 2 हफ्ते में tyre pressure check करें। Petrol pump पर free में होता है। सही pressure — vehicle manual में देखें।
Under-inflated tyres consume 5-10% more fuel. Check tyre pressure every 2 weeks. Done free at petrol pumps. Correct pressure — check vehicle manual.
2
Smooth Driving — Accelerate और Brake slowly
Smooth Driving — Accelerate and Brake Slowly
Sudden acceleration और hard braking 20-30% ज्यादा fuel खाते हैं। Smooth और steady speed maintain करें। Anticipate traffic — engine braking use करें।
Sudden acceleration and hard braking consume 20-30% more fuel. Maintain smooth and steady speed. Anticipate traffic — use engine braking.
3
AC का Use कम करें जहाँ possible हो
Reduce AC Use Where Possible
Car AC 5-15% ज्यादा fuel खाती है। City में slow traffic में windows खोलें। Highway पर windows बंद करें — aerodynamic drag कम होती है।
Car AC consumes 5-15% more fuel. In city slow traffic, open windows. On highway, keep windows closed — reduces aerodynamic drag.
4
Engine पर ध्यान दें
Maintain Your Engine
Dirty air filter से 10% ज्यादा fuel खर्च होता है। Regular servicing से mileage 10-15% improve हो सकती है। Spark plugs, oil filter — scheduled service पर change करें।
A dirty air filter causes 10% more fuel consumption. Regular servicing can improve mileage by 10-15%. Change spark plugs and oil filter at scheduled service.
5
Route Planning — Traffic बचाएं
Route Planning — Avoid Traffic
Google Maps से real-time traffic देखें। Traffic में idle engine 0 km/h पर fuel खाता रहता है। Peak hours avoid करें जहाँ possible हो — early morning या late evening।
Check real-time traffic on Google Maps. Idle engine in traffic still consumes fuel at 0 km/h. Avoid peak hours where possible — early morning or late evening.

Fuel Efficient Driving — जो पैसे बचाती है

Fuel Efficient Driving — That Saves Money

Same car, same petrol rate — लेकिन driving style से mileage 20-30% तक बदल सकती है।

Same car, same petrol rate — but driving style can change mileage by 20-30%.

🚗
Optimal Speed
Highway पर 60-80 km/h most fuel-efficient। 100+ km/h पर mileage dramatically गिरती है।
60-80 km/h is most fuel-efficient on highway. Mileage drops dramatically above 100 km/h.
⚙️
Gear Change
Manual: low RPM (2000-2500) पर higher gear। Over-revving करने से fuel waste होता है।
Manual: shift to higher gear at low RPM (2000-2500). Over-revving wastes fuel.
⚖️
Weight कम रखें
Extra 50 kg weight से 2% ज्यादा fuel। Boot में unnecessary weight न रखें।
Extra 50 kg weight increases fuel by 2%. Don't keep unnecessary weight in the boot.
🌬️
Aerodynamics
Roof rack, heavy accessories — aerodynamic drag बढ़ाते हैं। Highway पर windows बंद करें।
Roof rack, heavy accessories — increase aerodynamic drag. Keep windows closed on highway.
