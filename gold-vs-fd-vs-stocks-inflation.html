🥇 Gold vs FD vs Stocks — महंगाई में कहाँ लगाएं?Gold vs FD vs Stocks — Where to Invest During Inflation?

जब महंगाई बढ़े और रुपया कमजोर हो — तो पैसा कहाँ लगाएं? Gold, FD, या Mutual Fund?

When inflation rises and rupee weakens — where should you invest? Gold, FD, or Mutual Funds?

महंगाई के समय सबसे बड़ा financial question यह होता है — पैसा कहाँ रखें? Cash रखने पर inflation उसकी value घटाती है। लेकिन हर investment में अलग risk और return है।

During inflation, the biggest financial question is — where should I keep my money? Keeping cash means inflation erodes its value. But every investment has different risks and returns.

Disclaimer: यह educational information है — financial advice नहीं। कोई भी investment decision लेने से पहले SEBI registered financial advisor से सलाह लें।

Disclaimer: This is educational information — not financial advice. Consult a SEBI registered financial advisor before making any investment decisions.

हर Option को समझें

Understanding Each Option

🥇
Gold — Traditional Inflation Hedge
Gold — Traditional Inflation Hedge
Gold historically inflation के साथ बढ़ता है। जब currency weak हो तो gold की demand बढ़ती है। Digital gold, Sovereign Gold Bonds, या physical gold — तीनों options हैं। SGBs पर additional interest भी मिलता है। लेकिन short term में volatile हो सकता है।
Gold historically rises with inflation. When currency weakens, demand for gold increases. Options include digital gold, Sovereign Gold Bonds, or physical gold. SGBs also provide additional interest. However, gold can be volatile in the short term.
🏦
FD — Safe but Watch the Rate
FD — Safe but Watch the Rate
FD guaranteed return देता है। लेकिन अगर inflation rate FD interest rate से ज्यादा है, तो real returns negative हो जाते हैं। उदाहरण: FD 7% return दे और inflation 8% हो — तो real return -1%। Emergency fund के लिए FD अच्छा है।
FD gives guaranteed returns. But if inflation exceeds FD interest rate, real returns turn negative. Example: 7% FD return with 8% inflation means -1% real return. FD is good for emergency funds.
📈
Equity Mutual Funds — Long Term Winner
Equity Mutual Funds — Long Term Winner
Long term (5-10 years) में equity mutual funds inflation को beat करते हैं historically। Short term में market volatile होता है। SIP के जरिए छोटी amounts से invest करना शुरू करें। Inflation में SIP बंद करने की गलती न करें।
Historically, equity mutual funds beat inflation over the long term (5-10 years). Markets are volatile short-term. Start investing small amounts via SIP. Don't make the mistake of stopping SIP during inflation.

SmartBachao Approach: एक balanced approach — Emergency fund FD में, कुछ Gold में (Sovereign Gold Bonds), और long-term wealth के लिए Equity MF में SIP। Diversification सबसे smart strategy है। लेकिन exact allocation के लिए financial advisor से मिलें।

SmartBachao Approach: A balanced approach — emergency fund in FD, some gold (Sovereign Gold Bonds), and long-term wealth in equity MF via SIP. Diversification is the smartest strategy. But for exact allocation, consult a financial advisor.

Sovereign Gold Bond vs Physical Gold? +
Sovereign Gold Bond (SGB) government issue करती है। इस पर 2.5% सालाना interest extra मिलता है physical gold की appreciation के ऊपर। Storage की चिंता नहीं। Long-term investment के लिए SGB physical gold से बेहतर है।
Sovereign Gold Bonds (SGBs) are issued by the government. They provide an additional 2.5% annual interest on top of gold price appreciation. No storage worries. For long-term investment, SGBs are better than physical gold.
Inflation में क्या नहीं करना चाहिए?What to avoid during inflation? +
1) सारा पैसा cash में न रखें। 2) SIP बंद न करें। 3) Panic में कोई बड़ा investment decision न लें। 4) High-interest loans न लें। 5) FOMO में cryptocurrency या risky assets में सब कुछ न लगाएं।
1) Don't keep all money in cash. 2) Don't stop SIPs. 3) Don't make panicked investment decisions. 4) Don't take high-interest loans. 5) Don't put everything in cryptocurrency or risky assets due to FOMO.
