घर के खर्चे कम करें

Reduce Household Expenses

1
Bijli bill smart करें
Cut electricity bill smartly
AC temperature 24-26°C रखें, LED बल्ब लगाएं, standby devices बंद करें। हमारी bijli bill guide देखें।
Set AC to 24-26°C, install LED bulbs, switch off standby devices. Read our bijli bill guide.
2
PNG connection लें (अगर available हो)
Get PNG connection (if available)
जब gas महंगी हो, PNG एक better option हो सकता है। One-time investment, long-term savings।
When gas is expensive, PNG can be a better option. One-time investment, long-term savings.
3
Meal planning करें
Plan your meals
Weekly menu पहले बनाएं, फिर उसी का सामान लाएं। Wastage कम होती है, खर्च भी।
Plan your weekly menu first, then shop exactly for that. Less wastage, less spending.
4
घर में सब्जी उगाएं
Grow vegetables at home
Terrace या balcony में धनिया, पालक, मिर्च उगाना start करें। हमारी garden guide देखें।
Start growing coriander, spinach, chilli on terrace or balcony. Read our garden guide.
5
Transport smart करें
Optimize transport
Short distances के लिए cycle लें। Carpooling करें। जहाँ possible हो public transport use करें।
Get a cycle for short distances. Carpool to work. Use public transport where possible.
6
Unused subscriptions cancel करें
Cancel unused subscriptions
OTT, gym, apps — जो regularly use नहीं होती उन्हें cancel करें। उदाहरण के तौर पर ₹200-500/month बच सकते हैं।
OTT, gym, apps — cancel what you rarely use. Example savings: ₹200-500/month.
7
Bulk staples खरीदें
Buy staples in bulk
चावल, दाल, आटा — bulk में per kg सस्ते। Non-perishables ज्यादा लेने में समझदारी है।
Rice, dal, atta — cheaper per kg in bulk. Smart to buy more of non-perishables.
8
Branded vs Generic सोचें
Think branded vs generic
Staples में generic brands ठीक काम करते हैं। Branded premium सिर्फ वहाँ दें जहाँ फर्क पड़े।
Generic brands work fine for staples. Pay brand premium only where quality genuinely matters.
9
Emergency fund बनाएं
Build an emergency fund
3-6 महीने के खर्चे के बराबर emergency fund जरूर रखें। Inflation में अचानक बड़े खर्चे आते हैं।
Keep 3-6 months of expenses as an emergency fund. Inflation brings unexpected big expenses.
10
SIP जारी रखें
Keep SIP running
Inflation में SIP बंद करने की गलती न करें। Long term में mutual funds inflation से better returns दे सकते हैं।
Don't stop SIP during inflation. Long term, mutual funds can deliver returns better than inflation.

15 Practical तरीके — महंगाई में पैसे बचाने के

15 Practical Ways to Save Money During Inflation

1
50-30-20 Budget Rule अपनाएं
Adopt the 50-30-20 Budget Rule
50% जरूरी खर्च (किराया, राशन, बिजली, EMI), 30% इच्छाएं (खाना बाहर, shopping), 20% बचत (FD, RD, SIP)। पहले बचत करें — बाकी में जो बचे वह खर्च करें।
50% necessities (rent, groceries, electricity, EMI), 30% wants (eating out, shopping), 20% savings (FD, RD, SIP). Save first — spend what remains.
2
Grocery में Smart Shopping
Smart Grocery Shopping
List बनाकर जाएं — बिना list 20-30% extra खर्च होता है। Seasonal सब्जियाँ लें — सबसे सस्ती और ताज़ी। Dal, चावल, आटा bulk में खरीदें — per kg सस्ता पड़ता है। Local मंडी vs supermarket — मंडी में 30-50% सस्ता।
Always shop with a list — without one you spend 20-30% more. Buy seasonal vegetables — cheapest and freshest. Buy dal, rice, flour in bulk — cheaper per kg. Local mandi vs supermarket — mandi is 30-50% cheaper.
3
Energy Bills — AC और Bijli
Energy Bills — AC and Electricity
AC 24°C पर रखें — 6% electricity बचती है per degree। LED bulbs — old CFL से replace करें। Standby devices बंद करें रात को। इन तीन changes से उदाहरण के तौर पर ₹500-800/month बच सकते हैं।
Keep AC at 24°C — saves 6% electricity per degree. Replace old CFLs with LED bulbs. Switch off standby devices at night. These three changes can save approximately ₹500-800/month.
4
Transport — Cycle, Metro, Carpool
Transport — Cycle, Metro, Carpool
Short distances के लिए cycle। Metro route पर है तो car छोड़ें। Same direction colleagues के साथ carpool। 2 WFH days per week = 8 दिन का petrol बचत। Combined savings उदाहरण के तौर पर ₹3,000-5,000/month।
Cycle for short distances. Take metro if on the route. Carpool with colleagues going the same way. 2 WFH days per week = 8 days of fuel saved. Combined savings approximately ₹3,000-5,000/month.
5
Subscription Audit — जो use नहीं वो बंद करें
Subscription Audit — Cancel What You Don't Use
Netflix, Amazon Prime, Hotstar, Spotify, gym membership — list बनाएं सब की। Last 30 दिन में कितनी बार use किया? अगर 5 से कम बार — cancel करें। Average Indian के ₹800-1,500/month यहाँ waste होते हैं।
Netflix, Amazon Prime, Hotstar, Spotify, gym membership — list them all. How many times used in last 30 days? If under 5 times — cancel. Average Indian wastes ₹800-1,500/month on unused subscriptions.
6
Eating Out कम करें — Home Meal Prep
Reduce Eating Out — Home Meal Prep
एक बार restaurant में खाना = 5-7 घर के खाने का खर्च। Weekly meal prep करें — Sunday को 2-3 dishes batch cook करें। Zomato/Swiggy की habit महंगी है। महीने में 4 बार घर पर खाना cook करें = ₹1,500-3,000 बचत।
One restaurant meal = cost of 5-7 home meals. Do weekly meal prep — batch cook 2-3 dishes on Sunday. Zomato/Swiggy habit is expensive. Cook at home 4 more times per month = ₹1,500-3,000 savings.
7
UPI Cashback और Offers का फायदा लें
Use UPI Cashback and Offers
PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm पर regular cashback offers आते हैं। Grocery, fuel, bill payment पर। Smart use से ₹200-500/month extra बच सकते हैं। Credit card reward points को redeem करें — expire न होने दें।
PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm regularly have cashback offers on groceries, fuel, bill payments. Smart use can save ₹200-500/month extra. Redeem credit card reward points — don't let them expire.
8
Generic vs Brand — Smart choice करें
Generic vs Brand — Make Smart Choices
Medicines में generic (same molecule, same effect, 60-80% सस्ती) लें — doctor से generic prescription माँगें। Grocery में house brand/store brand try करें। Cleaning products में — vinegar + baking soda बहुत कुछ clean कर सकता है branded cleaner से कम खर्च में।
For medicines, buy generic (same molecule, same effect, 60-80% cheaper) — ask doctor for generic prescription. Try house brand/store brand for groceries. For cleaning — vinegar + baking soda can clean most things at lower cost than branded cleaners.
9
Emergency Fund — पहले बनाएं
Emergency Fund — Build First
3-6 months के खर्चे जितना emergency fund होना चाहिए। इसके बिना कोई भी unexpected expense (medical, car repair, job loss) debt में ले जाती है। High-yield savings account या liquid mutual fund में रखें। SIP से शुरू करें — ₹500/month भी।
Emergency fund should cover 3-6 months of expenses. Without it, any unexpected expense (medical, car repair, job loss) pushes into debt. Keep in high-yield savings or liquid mutual fund. Start with SIP — even ₹500/month.
10
Second-hand खरीदें — जहाँ possible हो
Buy Second-hand — Where Possible
Books, furniture, cycle, electronics — OLX और Facebook Marketplace पर 40-70% सस्ते मिलते हैं। Baby items especially — बच्चे जल्दी बड़े होते हैं, second-hand में कोई फर्क नहीं पड़ता।
Books, furniture, cycle, electronics — available 40-70% cheaper on OLX and Facebook Marketplace. Baby items especially — children grow quickly, second-hand makes no difference.
11
Mobile और Internet Plans Review करें
Review Mobile and Internet Plans
क्या आप जितना data use करते हैं उतना pay कर रहे हैं? Airtel, Jio, Vi — plans compare करें। Family में एक plan share करें। WiFi घर पर हो तो कम data plan काफी है। Annual recharge usually monthly से 15-20% सस्ता।
Are you paying for more data than you use? Compare Airtel, Jio, Vi plans. Share one plan in the family. If you have WiFi at home, a lower data plan is enough. Annual recharge is usually 15-20% cheaper than monthly.
12
SIP से Inflation को Beat करें
Beat Inflation with SIP
FD 6-7% return देती है लेकिन inflation 5-6% है — real return बहुत कम। Equity mutual funds long term में 10-15% return देते हैं — inflation को beat करते हैं। छोटे से ₹500/month SIP start करें — compounding का magic देखें।
FD gives 6-7% return but inflation is 5-6% — real return is very low. Equity mutual funds give 10-15% return long-term — beats inflation. Start a small ₹500/month SIP — see the magic of compounding.
13
Home Garden — सब्जियाँ खुद उगाएं
Home Garden — Grow Your Own Vegetables
धनिया, पुदीना, मिर्च — balcony में उगाएं। साल में ₹3,000-6,000 बचत। Chemical-free खाना bonus है। हमारी detailed guide देखें।
Coriander, mint, chilli — grow on balcony. Annual savings of ₹3,000-6,000. Chemical-free food is a bonus. See our detailed guide.
14
High-interest Debt पहले खत्म करें
Pay Off High-Interest Debt First
Credit card का outstanding 36-42% annual interest लेता है — यह सबसे expensive debt है। हर महीने full payment करें — minimum payment trap में मत पड़ें। Personal loan (14-24%) भी जल्दी close करें।
Credit card outstanding charges 36-42% annual interest — the most expensive debt. Pay full amount every month — don't fall into the minimum payment trap. Close personal loans (14-24%) quickly too.
15
Government Schemes का फायदा लें
Take Advantage of Government Schemes
PM Surya Ghar (solar subsidy), PM Jan Dhan (zero balance account), Ayushman Bharat (health insurance), PMAY (housing subsidy) — आपके eligible होने के बावजूद कितनी schemes miss कर रहे हैं? Check करें।
PM Surya Ghar (solar subsidy), PM Jan Dhan (zero balance account), Ayushman Bharat (health insurance), PMAY (housing subsidy) — how many schemes are you missing despite being eligible? Check now.
